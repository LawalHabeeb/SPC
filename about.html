WHO ARE WE

SPC is leading indigenous firm that provides industry focused assurance, tax and advisory services to build public trust and enhance value for its clients and their stakeholders. We guide our clients through business challenges by understanding their needs and bringing together the right team to address them. With experience in-house professionals and associates we can meet your needs.


SPC is an organization committed to helping its clients meet the challenges posed by global economy. We are rich and vast in local and global business knowledge, we possess an enviable breadth and depth of resources with international trainings and knowledge exposure, yet we work locally, bringing appropriate local knowledge and experience to bear and using the depth of our resources to provide a professional service, specifically tailored to meet our clients’ needs. The service we offer to clients is underpinned by our nimbleness, extensive coverage and breadth of skills.

If I could summarize in one sentence what SPC represents, it would be the blend of technical excellence and sector expertise, combined with a personal commitment from everyone within the firm to foster professional working relationships with our clients enabling us to be your long-term partner in business

Strategy Pros & Cons (SPC)

The SPC brand is based on the integration of three concepts – People, Knowledge and Value: the experience and rich cultural diversity of our people, the boundless knowledge that flows within our organization and between ourselves and our clients, and the value that give context and meaning both to our people and to our clients.
