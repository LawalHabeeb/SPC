WHO ARE WE

SPC is leading indigenous firm that provides industry focused assurance, tax and advisory services to build public trust and enhance value for its clients and their stakeholders. We guide our clients through business challenges by understanding their needs and bringing together the right team to address them. With experience in-house professionals and associates we can meet your needs.


SPC is an organization committed to helping its clients meet the challenges posed by global economy. We are rich and vast in local and global business knowledge, we possess an enviable breadth and depth of resources with international trainings and knowledge exposure, yet we work locally, bringing appropriate local knowledge and experience to bear and using the depth of our resources to provide a professional service, specifically tailored to meet our clients’ needs. The service we offer to clients is underpinned by our nimbleness, extensive coverage and breadth of skills.

Strategy Pros & Cons (SPC)

The SPC brand is based on the integration of three concepts – People, Knowledge and Value: the experience and rich cultural diversity of our people, the boundless knowledge that flows within our organization and between ourselves and our clients, and the value that give context and meaning both to our people and to our clients. The power of our brand only reaches its full potential when these elements come together.

SPC’S ambition is to be the world’s leading professional services organization, thereby solving complex business problems for top-tier clients in global, national and local markets. In order to succeed in this ambition, we must grow and develop both as individuals and as a business.

Our core values of Excellence, Integrity, Leadership and Teamwork helps us to achieve this growth:
LIVINING OUR VALUES

In line with our purpose of building trust in society and solving important problems, our values support our strategy for future growth. While the core value of SPC remained consistent for a long time, we felt that there was an opportunity to refresh them to include a stronger focus on innovation and making a difference with all our stakeholders.

ACT WITH INTEGRITY
  • Speak up for what is right especially when it feels difficult
  • Expect and deliver the highest quality outcomes
  • Make decisions and act as if our personal reputations were at stake
MAKE A DIFFERENCE
  • Stay informed and ask questions about the future of the world we live in
  • Create impact with our colleagues, our clients and society through our actions
  • Embrace and Kaizen on technological changes and its destruction – Artificial Intelligence
  • Respond with agility to the ever–changing environment in which we operate
THE WAY WE DO BUSINESS

We have a code of Conduct for all SPC people. This code is based on our core values of Teamwork, Integrity, Leadership and Excellence. But knowing and understanding the code and values are not enough. We also have an obligation to comply with the letter, International Professional Practices Framework and its Professional Code of Ethics and help others do the same.

WORK TOGETHER
  • Collaborate and share relationships, ideas and knowledge beyond boundaries
  • Seek and integrate a diverse range of perspectives, people and ideas
  • Give and ask for feedback to improve ourselves and others
RECREATE THE POSSIBLE
  • Speak to challenge the status quo and try new things
  • Innovate, test and learn from failures
  • Embrace and Kaizen on technological changes and its destruction – Artificial Intelligence
  • Have an open mind to possibilities in every idea
